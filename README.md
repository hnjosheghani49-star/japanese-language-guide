## Study Tips for Beginners

If you are starting your Japanese learning journey, consider the following recommendations:

1. Learn Hiragana before anything else.
2. Practice Katakana regularly.
3. Build vocabulary every day.
4. Study grammar consistently.
5. Listen to native Japanese audio.
6. Read simple Japanese texts.
7. Create a realistic study schedule.
8. Review previously learned material frequently.
9. Prepare for JLPT examinations if certification is one of your goals.
10. Stay motivated and enjoy the learning process.
